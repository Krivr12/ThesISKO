Ensuring each Thesis are Stored for Future Iskolars

ThesISKO is the official thesis repository of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP), built to archive, preserve, and showcase student research across all disciplines. It serves as a digital library where students, faculty, and researchers can easily access a wide range of academic works produced within the university.

Mission and Goals

Our Mission

ThesISKO exists to make research accessible to everyone. We preserve the hard work of every student and give it a space to be discovered, cited, and shared. Guided by six pillars, we ensure access for all by making research easy to find and read; Preservation by providing a lasting home for student work; Recognition through clear authorship and proper citation; Integrity via ethical sharing, plagiarism awareness, and accurate metadata; Security & Consent by honoring privacy levels and authorsâ€™ choices; and Community by connecting students, faculty, and future researchers. By opening doors to knowledge, we help ideas travel farther connecting learners, empowering future projects, and recognizing the authors who make it possible.
