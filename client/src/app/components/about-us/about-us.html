Ensuring each Thesis are Stored for Future Iskolars

ThesISKO is the official thesis repository of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP), built to archive, preserve, and showcase student research across all disciplines. It serves as a digital library where students, faculty, and researchers can easily access a wide range of academic works produced within the university.
